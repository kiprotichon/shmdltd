About Shamida Kenya

Shamida Kenya Limited is a leading exporter of premium fruits and vegetables from Kenya, with a strong focus on avocados. We work directly with local farmers to ensure consistent quality, sustainability, and freshness from farm to market.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality avocados and produce to our global customers while empowering local farmers and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.

Our Values

  • Quality: We ensure the highest standards in our produce.
  • Sustainability: Committed to eco-friendly farming practices.
  • Integrity: Building trust through transparent operations.
  • Community: Supporting local farmers and communities.

Shamida Kenya Ltd.

Exporting the freshest avocados and produce from Kenya to the world.
